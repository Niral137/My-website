Tim Berners-Lee is a software engineer who invented the internet or the World Wide Web in 1989 while at CERN. He is the director of World Wide Web Consortium that he founded and a professor at the computer science department at the University of Oxford.In 2001 he became a Fellow of the Royal Society and in 2004 he was knighted
In 1994 Berners-Lee founded the W3C at the Laboratory of Computer Science at the Massachusetts Institute of Technology in Boston. It was made up of companies that wanted to work together to create Web standards andalso improve the Web. It was quickly agreed that the technology should be free of royalties and pantents. Berners-Lee served as director of this W3C since it was founded.In 1995 in Microsoft produced the first version of Internet Explorer and this comercial brower created enormous growth in websites and web users.The Web was first was used for E-commerce in 1997
